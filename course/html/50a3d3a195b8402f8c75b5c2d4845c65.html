Open edX is a learner-centric, massively scalable learning platform behind edX.org. Open edX is a leading platform for MOOC, Higher Ed, enterprise, and government organizations alike.

edX is the online learning destination co-founded by Harvard and MIT. The edX team created and currently maintains Open edX with a large community built around it.

One major component of Open edX is the platform or more commonly known as the edx-platform. It is the largest component containing both learner and educator tools known as the Learning Management System (LMS) and Studio respectively.
